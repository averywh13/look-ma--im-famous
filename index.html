WHO WAS LEONARDO DA VINCI?

Leonardo da Vinci, born 1452 and passed 1519, was an Italian influence of the Renaissance period, known for his findings in various fields such painting, inventing, scientific, and many more. His talents and contributions have made him one of the most iconic figures in the history of art. Da Vinci's legacy lives not only through his completed works of art but in his numerous sketches and writings that give insight into his creative processes and intellectual pursuits. His ability to integrate artistic expression with scientific inquiry has left a superficial mark on the history of human achievement.
